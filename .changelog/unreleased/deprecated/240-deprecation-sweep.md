* Deprecate the mixed-denom wire surface, which is now inert: `payment_denom` on `MsgCreateVaultRequest` and `VaultAccount` (including the `uylds.fcc` NAV-check exemption), and the `redeem_denom` selection on `MsgSwapOutRequest` and `QueryEstimateSwapOutRequest`. The fields remain on the wire for client compatibility; deletion is deferred to a future major release. The never-released `initial_payment_nav` field and `InitialVaultNAV` message are removed outright with their field number reserved [#240](https://github.com/provlabs/vault/issues/240).
