* Deprecate the payment-denom and direct-principal API surface ahead of single-denom vaults: `payment_denom` and `initial_payment_nav` on `MsgCreateVaultRequest`, `payment_denom` on `VaultAccount` (including the `uylds.fcc` NAV-check exemption), `redeem_denom` selection on `MsgSwapOutRequest` and `QueryEstimateSwapOutRequest`, and the `DepositPrincipalFunds`/`WithdrawPrincipalFunds` endpoints with their events. Direct principal mutation will be reworked into the exchange-backed payments flow (`AcceptAsset`/`RejectAsset`); the deprecated surface will be removed in a later release [#240](https://github.com/provlabs/vault/issues/240).
